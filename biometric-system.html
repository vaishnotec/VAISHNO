Biometric System

Advanced biometric attendance and access control solutions for secure workplaces and institutions.

Move beyond manual registers and insecure punch cards. Vaishno Tech deploys biometric systems that reliably capture fingerprints, RFID cards or face recognition to record attendance and manage entry into restricted zones.

  • RFID / PIN / face recognition options

  • Cloud or on‑premise time‑tracking software
