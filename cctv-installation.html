CCTV Surveillance

End‑to‑end CCTV planning, installation and configuration for homes, shops and enterprises.

We design CCTV solutions that cover blind spots, record in high quality and provide reliable remote access from anywhere. Our engineers consider lighting, entry points and cable routes to place cameras for maximum visibility and deterrence.

From standalone DVRs to IP‑based NVR systems, we integrate CCTV with your existing network and secure access with strong passwords and role‑based permissions, ensuring evidence is always available when needed.

  • High‑resolution night‑vision recording
  • Mobile apps for live view and playback
  • Cloud / local backup and storage planning
  • Maintenance, relocation and upgrade services
