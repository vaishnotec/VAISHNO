Hardware Repair

Component‑level repair for desktops, laptops, printers and networking equipment.

Our hardware team is equipped to handle complex faults across PCs, printers and network devices. Instead of suggesting full replacement immediately, we first analyze what can be economically repaired or refurbished.

We maintain a strong focus on ESD safety, accurate diagnosis and transparent communication so you always know what is being replaced and why.

  • Motherboard, RAM, storage and PSU replacement
  • Printer servicing and part replacement
  • Fan, cooler and thermal management issues
  • Power, boot and random restart problems
  • On‑site visits for business‑critical systems
