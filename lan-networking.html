LAN Networking

Professional design and deployment of secure, high‑speed local networks for offices, institutions and homes.

Vaishno Tech delivers robust LAN networking solutions that keep your teams always connected. We begin with a detailed site survey to understand your layout, cabling routes and device density, then engineer a topology that balances performance, security and future scalability.

From rack dressing and structured cabling to L2 & L3 switch configuration, VLANs, QoS and network security, we implement industry best practices so your critical applications run smoothly without downtime or bottlenecks.

  • Structured CAT6 / CAT6A cabling and fiber backbone
  • Managed and unmanaged switch deployment
  • Segmentation via VLANs, guest networks and access control
  • Wi‑Fi access point planning and roaming optimization
  • Network documentation and labeling for easy maintenance
